Privacy Policy for Watch Fido

Last updated: September 7, 2024

At Watch Fido, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide when using our app, which is designed to help you manage and organize your pets' care information. With your permission, this app accesses certain personal data, including your calendar, messages, and photos, to provide these functionalities. We are committed to ensuring your information is safe and used appropriately.

1. Information We Collect

Watch Fido may collect the following types of information from users, depending on the permissions you grant:

  • Calendar Data: We access your calendar to help you create and manage important pet-related events such as vet appointments and medication schedules.
  • Messages: We may allow you to share PDF reports or pet care details via email or SMS, but we do not read, store, or monitor your messages.
  • Photos: If you choose to add photos of your pets to their profiles, we will request access to your photo library to select images. We only access the photos you select and do not access, store, or use any other images from your library.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide and improve the core functionality of Watch Fido. Specifically:

  • Calendar Access: To create pet care events and appointments that you can manage through your device’s calendar.
  • Message Access: To enable you to share pet reports or health updates via email or SMS. We do not retain or store message content.
  • Photos: To allow you to personalize your pet’s and parent’s profile with images from your photo library.

3. Data Storage and Security

All user data is stored locally on your device using Core Data. We do not store or back up sensitive information on external servers, nor do we use encryption for the data stored locally. Since all data is stored on your device, you are responsible for the security and backup of this information.

We take the following measures to ensure that data remains secure on your device:

  • Your data remains private as it is stored locally and is not transmitted or stored externally.
  • Watch Fido does not have access to your data once stored on your device.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not share or sell your data to third parties. All data is stored locally on your device, and we do not have the ability to access or share your information.

Note: Sharing reports or information is entirely user-initiated, and Watch Fido does not process, store, or transmit this information beyond your device.

5. Third-Party Services

Watch Fido does not integrate with any third-party services that collect or process user data without your explicit consent. Any third-party services that may be introduced will be clearly communicated, and you will have the option to enable or disable their functionality.

6. Your Choices and Controls

You can control and manage the permissions Watch Fido has access to via your device settings. This includes the ability to:

  • Enable or disable access to your calendar, contacts, messages, and photos.

Please note that since all data is stored locally on your device, you are responsible for managing and deleting this information as needed. Watch Fido does not have the ability to access or delete your data.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app, and we will notify you of any significant changes that affect your rights or how we use your information.
