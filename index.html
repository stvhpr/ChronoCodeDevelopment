Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I add a new pet to Watch Fido?
Adding a new pet is easy! Simply go to the main dashboard, tap the "Add Pet" button, and fill in your pet’s details. You can manage multiple pets all in one app.

Can I share my pet’s care instructions with others?
Yes! You can share your pet’s feeding schedules, medications, and emergency contacts via text or email. Perfect for when you're out of town or need to provide care instructions to a sitter or family member. Select "View Pets" to share via text, or select "Send Details" to create a PDF that can be shared via email.

How do I generate a PDF report?
To generate a PDF report with your pet's details, head to the "Reports" section, select whether to include the health details or not, and tap the “Generate PDF” button. You can share this report with vets, sitters, or friends instantly.

How do I set reminders for vet appointments or treatments?
Watch Fido integrates with your calendar to ensure you never miss an important date. Add events to your calendar and set reminders within the calendar appointment.

How do I update my pet's health information?
Select the "Health" section, where you can log details about vaccinations, medications, allergies, and vet visits.

Can I store my vet’s contact information in the app?
Yes! You can save all relevant contact information for your vet, groomer, or pet sitters. Their contact details will be just a tap away whenever you need them.

Watch Fido Features Overview

  • Share Care Instructions: Quickly share care schedules with pet sitters via text or email.
  • Health Tracking: Monitor vaccines, medications, vet visits, and more.
  • PDF Reports: Generate and share comprehensive care reports with a single click.
  • Reminders: Set up automatic reminders for appointments and treatments.
  • Multiple Pets: Manage all your pets in one app, whether you have dogs, cats, or even reptiles.
  • Contact Storage: Keep all important vet and caretaker contacts in one place.
