Getting Started

Main Menu Magic

Ready to begin? When you open the app, you’ll land on the Start Screen—your gateway to mastering microbiology. Here’s what you can do:

  • Start: Ready to test your knowledge? Let’s go!
  • Manage Flashcards: Your deck, your rules—add, edit, or delete flashcards to tailor your study experience.
  • Information: That’s where you are now! Get the scoop on app features, version info, and best practices for studying effectively.

Using the Flashcards

Reviewing Flashcards

Let’s start the fun! Tap Start on the Start Screen, and you’ll dive straight into the world of microbiology flashcards. Here’s how it works:

  • You will be presented with a question.
  • Reveal the answer with a simple tap—did you get it right?
  • Swipe left to move to the next flashcard or swipe right to revisit the previous one.
  • Alternatively, you can use the Next and Previous buttons at the bottom to move through the deck.
  • Focus your study by using the Category Filter at the top to select a specific topic (e.g., Bacteriology, Mycology, Virology). Hone in on what matters most!

Managing Flashcards

Adding New Flashcards

Want to add your own questions? Here’s how:

  • Tap Manage Flashcards from the Start Screen.
  • Select Add New Cards.
  • Fill in the Question and Answer fields and choose a Category. Need a new category? You can create that too!
  • Tap Save Card to add it to your collection—voilà, it’s ready!

Editing Existing Flashcards

Need to tweak a card? No problem:

  • Go to Manage Flashcards and select Edit Cards.
  • Choose the flashcard you want to update.
  • Make your changes—edit the Question, Answer, or Category.
  • Tap Save Changes to update it, or hit Delete Flashcard if you want it gone for good.

Tips for Effective Study

Make your study sessions count with these tips:

  • Consistent Review: Use the app regularly to reinforce what you’ve learned.
  • Focused Study: Use the category filter to zoom in on topics where you need more practice.
  • Active Recall: Try answering each question before revealing the answer to strengthen memory retention.
  • Self-Assessment: Keep track of which flashcards challenge you the most and review them more frequently.
